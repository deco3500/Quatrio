What Versi Does?


INSPECT

We inspect articles about controversial topics from multiple sites.

We collate the facts and present them to you.



DISCUSS

We share the facts for you to discuss with like-minded people.

DEBATE

We host debates for each topic so you can voice your opnions and hear what others think as well.

COMMUNITY

We provide a safe community for you to express your opinions on controversial topics without fear of being judged.
