Pitch

Versi is a mobile-based web page/app which addresses the issue of bias in controversial topics in the media. It is primarily a discussion/debate forum where users are able to somewhat freely express their opinions but in a more professional manner. Bias in the media often leads to misinformation of major news topics, which may generally spark a massive division in society. Versi's unique core feature make a fun and interesting learning environment for users looking to expand their knowledge. The first is that the editors take facts from the news and collate articles reporting on the new stories and present them to the users. This provides a completely unbiased view of the issue and allows users to construct their own arguments. These issues follow heavy and controversial topics regarding human rights. Versi conducts polls and allows commenting sections for discussion on the issues. A core feature of Versi is that it allows users to conduct live debates against other users. The Versi web application puts the users in teams to debate a human rights topic. Allowing user to even debate form a perspective that they may not even side with, expanding their knowledge of the topic and skills in speech and debate.
